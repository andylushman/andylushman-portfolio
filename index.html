About Me

Educator Turned Programmer

Follow your curiosity. This is what led me to programming. What led me to every new adventure my life has had in store for me, in fact. When, as a career educator, my heart told me I was ready for something new, I remembered this advice: follow your curiosity. I had some background with technology and an interest that turned into a curiosity. That curiosity led me to teach myself the fundamentals of programming with help from friends who are developers, online resources, and eventually led me to enroll in the University of Denver’s Coding Bootcamp. I am ready to follow my curiosity into what the tech world has to offer.

Other places my curiosity has led me: a ten-year career in Physical Education; living and teaching on three continents and traveling the world; a masters in International Education, earning a black belt in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu; mountain biking for fun with my wife and friends.

I have a unique combination of curiosity and persistence; I am a problem-solver and have an acute attention to detail. I look forward to utilizing these skills in the field of technology.
