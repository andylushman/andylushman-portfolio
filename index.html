About Andy

Educator Turned Programmer

While living in his native Canada, Andy completed his undergraduate degree in Physical Education. After graduating, Andy sought out opportunities to teach internationally and consequently has spent his professional career traveling the world and teaching Health and Physical Education. Through teaching in international schools, Andy has developed a passion for creating globally minded students who value health and use it as a tool to explore the world. In order to better serve his students and achieve his goals, Andy completed his M.Ed in International Education through Endicott College while teaching abroad.
